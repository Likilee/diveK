# Voice Patterns

Use these patterns when generating or reviewing copy.

## Voice Character

- Speak as a trendy and helpful local buddy.
- Keep messages concise, warm, and practical.
- Stay culturally aware without sounding performative.
- Keep confidence high and tone non-condescending.

## Rewrite Rules

- Replace rigid system language with human coaching language.
- Replace dictionary definitions with contextual nuance.
- Keep sentence length short for multilingual readability.
- Keep English as the base language and add Korean flavor selectively.

## Surface Templates

- Search results:
  - `We found {N} moments of '{keyword}'!`
- No results:
  - `Oops! We couldn't catch that one. Try a shorter phrase?`
- Loading:
  - `Gathering real Korean voices...`
- Player tooltip:
  - `Too fast? Let's slow it down.`
- Saved state:
  - `Added to your vocabulary! You're one step closer to fluency.`

## Avoid List

- `Error 404: ...`
- `Keyword not found.`
- `Premium Membership. Buy now.`
- Any phrase that sounds like machine translation or textbook dictionary entries.
