## 1. Brand Core

### "Textbook to Real Life"

DiveK delivers **living, breathing Korean context** --- not frozen,
textbook-style language.\
We move beyond dry dictionary definitions and create a premium space
where global learners can absorb real Korean nuance in the most
immersive way possible.

### Mission

Help Korean learners worldwide quickly and intuitively master real-life
nuance through K-content.

### Vision

To become the global No.1 Korean context search engine --- permanently
pinned on the browsers of K-culture fans and Korean learners.

### Core Values

1.  **Authenticity**\
    No robotic TTS. No artificial example sentences.\
    Only raw, native Korean speech.

2.  **Immersion**\
    No visual clutter. No banner ads.\
    Nothing interrupts the flow of watching and shadowing.

3.  **Context First**\
    Not just "This means X."\
    But "This is when and how you say it --- and in what tone."

## 3. Visual Identity

We completely reject childish language-app aesthetics (primary colors,
mascots) and predictable red/blue Korean flag themes.

services like Netflix and Spotify.

### UI/UX Concept

`Minimalist` + `Cinematic`

Like early Google, the home screen centers around one bold search bar
--- maximum clarity, zero distraction.

## 4. Tone of Voice

The primary UI language is **English**, but we subtly blend in Korean
charm when appropriate.

### Writing Principles

1.  **No Dictionary Speak**\
2.  **Encourage Progress --- but Make it Cool**\
3.  **Clear & Simple**\
    Always remember: English may not be the user's first language.
